<b>How to <i>Succeed</i> with WebWork</b>
<ul>
	<li> Write down the problem, or print the whole assignment using the "Download PDF" button. 
	<li> Answer the question <i>by hand on paper</i>.  Save your work, so that you can use it when you <b>study for the exam</b>.  
	<li> If your answer is incorrect, re-check your work.  
        <li> If you're still stumped, click the <b>ask the instructor button</b>. Explain what you're trying to do, and what you are stumped on. 
</ul>
