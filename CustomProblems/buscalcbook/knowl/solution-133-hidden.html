To find the exact velocity (IROC) at \(t=5\text{,}\) read the table from top to bottom, since the smaller values of \(\Delta t\) are further down in the table.

As you read the \(\frac{\Delta s}{\Delta t}\) column, note that it is getting closer and closer to \(75\text{.}\) In other words, at \(t=5\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{ds}{dt} = \lim_{\Delta t\rightarrow 0} \dfrac{\Delta s}{\Delta t} = 75 \frac{\text{miles}}{\text{hour}} \end{equation*}
