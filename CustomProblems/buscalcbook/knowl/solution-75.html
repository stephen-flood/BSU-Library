Solution 3.6.d.1.

To break even is to have exactly zero profit \(P(x)=0\text{.}\) That means solving the equation

\begin{equation*} -0.1x^2 + 18x - 500 = 0 \end{equation*}

You can solve this equation using the quadratic equation or by factoring. This function does not have a nice factorization, so we use the quadratic equation

\begin{equation*} x = \dfrac{ -18 \pm \sqrt{18^2 - 4\cdot (-0.1)\cdot (-500)} }{2\cdot (-1)} \end{equation*}

This gives us \(x=34.32\dots\) or \(x=145.68\)
