There are two parts of this graph

  1. When \(f\) is positive, then the ground is above the water. In this case, dry land is the region enclosed between \(f\) and the \(x\)-axis

    In this case, we say that \(f\) encloses positive net area.

  2. When \(f\) is negative, then the water is above the ground. In this case water is the region enclosed between \(f\) and the \(x\)-axis

    In this case, we say that \(f\) encloses negative net area.
