To define functions with jumps, we often use piecewise functions. The graph in ExplorationÂ 6.2 is made up of the following three functions:

  • When \(x\lt 10\text{,}\) graph the line \(50 + 5x\)

  • When \(x = 10\text{,}\) graph the point \(50 + 5x\)

  • When \(x\gt 10\text{,}\) graph the line \(90 + 6x\)

Mathematically, this is often abbreviated as

\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} 50 + 5x & \text{ if } x\lt 10 \\ 125 & \text{ if } x = 10 \\ 90 + 6x & \text{ if } x\gt 10 \\ \end{cases} \end{equation*}
