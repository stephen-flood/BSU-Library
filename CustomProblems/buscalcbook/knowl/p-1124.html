If \(c\) is a constant number,m and \(f(x)\) is any differentiable function,

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{d}{dx}\Big[c\cdot f(x) \Big] = c\cdot \dfrac{d}{dx}\Big[f(x)\Big] \end{equation*}

In bracket notation, this is

\begin{equation*} \Big[c \cdot f(x) \Big]' = c\cdot \Big[f(x)\Big]' \end{equation*}
