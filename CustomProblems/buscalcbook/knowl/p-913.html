Let \(y\) be any function of \(x\text{.}\) That means that any change of input \(\Delta x\) produces a change in the output \(\Delta y\text{.}\) If we average the change in outputs \(\Delta y\) over the inputs \(\Delta x\text{,}\) we get the average change \(\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}\text{.}\)
