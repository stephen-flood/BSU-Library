  • \(\displaystyle [{c}]'=0\)

  • \([{m\cdot x+b}]'=m\) and \([x]' = 1 \)

  • \(\displaystyle [{x^m}]'=m\cdot x^{m-1}\)

  • \(\displaystyle [{ c\cdot f(x)}]'=c\cdot [f(x)]'\)

  • \(\displaystyle [{f(x) \pm g(x)}]' = [{f(x)}]' \pm [{g(x)}]'\)
