Solution 3.6.c.1.

In the scenario (possible universe) where \(x=100\) items are sold, the total profit (which includes all revenue minus all cost) will be

\begin{equation*} P(100) = -0.1 (100)^2 + 18 (100) - 500 = 300 \end{equation*}

In the scenario (possible universe) where \(x=0\) items are sold, the total profit (which includes all revenue minus all cost) will be

\begin{equation*} P(0) = -0.1 (0)^2 + 18 (0) - 500 = -500 \end{equation*}
