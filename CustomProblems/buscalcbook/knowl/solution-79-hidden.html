Be very careful to follow the order of operations PEMDAS.

\begin{equation*} f(-1) = 5\cdot 0.75^{-1} = 5 \cdot 1.333\dots = 6.666\dots \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(0) = 5\cdot 0.75^{0} = 5 \cdot 1 = 5 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(1) = 5\cdot 0.75^{1} = 5 \cdot 0.75 = 3.75 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(2) = 5\cdot 0.75^{2} = 5 \cdot 0.5625 = 2.8125 \end{equation*}
