We can now combine the total revenue and total cost functions to get the profit function.

\begin{align*} \text{Profit} \amp = \text{Revenue} - \text{Cost} \\ P(x) \amp = R(x) - C(x) \\ \amp = ( -0.1x^2 + 21x) - (500 + 3x) \\ \amp = -0.1x^2 + 21x - 500 - 3x\\ P(x) \amp = -0.1x^2 + 18x - 500 \end{align*}
