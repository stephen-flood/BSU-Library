  • If \(n\neq -1\text{,}\) the general antiderivative of \(f(x)=x^n\) is \(F(x) = \dfrac{1}{n+1}x^{n+1} + C \)

  • The general antiderivative of \(f(x)=\frac{1}{x}\) is \(F(x) = \ln(|x|) + C \)

  • The general antiderivative of \(f(x)=e^x\) is \(F(x) = e^x + C \)

  • Suppose that \(a\) is a constant number. The general antiderivative of \(f(x)=a\) is \(F(x) = ax + C\text{.}\)
