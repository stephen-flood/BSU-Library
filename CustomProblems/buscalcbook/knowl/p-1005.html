Compute the average rate of change \(\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}\) over the intervals \([3,3.1]\text{,}\) \([3,3.01]\text{,}\) and \([3,3.001]\text{.}\) In other words, we have the \(x\) values, so we need to find the \(y\) values, the changes \(\Delta x\) and \(\Delta y\text{,}\) and then divide those changes to get the average change \(\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}\text{.}\)
