But we can rewrite the equation \(p\geq 0\) as follows:

\begin{equation*} p(x) \geq 0 \\ -0.1x + 21 \geq 0 \\ 21 \geq 0.1x \\ 210 \geq x \end{equation*}

So the item price function makes sense when \(0\leq x\) and \(x\leq 210\text{.}\) In other words, the applied domain is the interval \([0,210]\)
