Definition 12.8. Real Life Restricts Quanitites.

Let \(f\) be anu function and let \(l,r\) be any numbers.

The restriction of \(f(x)\) to the interval \([l,r]\) is the function that you get if you cut off the parts of the graph of the function that are less than \(l\) and greater than \(r\text{.}\)
