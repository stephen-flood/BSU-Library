Solution 4.5.c.1.

We want to rewrite \(3 P\) in the form \(P(1+r)\) for some number \(r\text{.}\) Here we have

\begin{equation*} 3P = P(3) = P(1 +2) \end{equation*}

In other words, the principal \(P\) has been increased by a decimal rate of \(2\text{,}\) which corresponds to an increased of \(200 \)%.
