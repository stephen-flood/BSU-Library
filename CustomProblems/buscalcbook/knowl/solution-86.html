Solution 4.5.a.1.

We want to rewrite \(1.1 P\) in the form \(P(1+r)\) for some number \(r\text{.}\) Here we have

\begin{equation*} 1.1P = P(1.1) = P(1 + 0.1) \end{equation*}

In other words, the principal \(P\) has been increased by a decimal rate of \(0.1\text{,}\) which corresponds to an increase of \(10 \)%.
