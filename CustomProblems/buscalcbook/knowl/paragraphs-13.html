Beautiful Fact.

The total profit function has a local maximum or minimum at the quantity \(c\) if and only if the marginal profit function \(P'\) goes between \((+)\) and \((-)\) at \(c\text{.}\)

Recall that the derivative \(P'\) and change function \(\Delta P\) are closely related:

  • Total profit is increasing when the change in profit is positive

  • Total profit is decreasing when the change in profit is negative

This means we can illustrate our method for finding maxima and minima using total and change graphs.
