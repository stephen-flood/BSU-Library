We want to find the price \(p(x)=-0.4x+10\) when \(x=18\text{.}\) In other words, substitute \(18\) for every occurance of \(x\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} p(18) = -0.4\cdot 18 + 10 = -7.2 + 10 = 2.8 \end{equation*}

A price of \(p=2.8\) $/item will result in 18 items being sold.
