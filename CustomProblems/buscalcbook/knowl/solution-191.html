Solution 12.2.b.1.

Graphing the function we see

Looking at the graph, we can see that there is a local maxima at \(x=1\) and a local minima at \(x=-1\text{.}\) We also know that there are no other locations where there might be a maxima or minima, even off the screen of the calculator.

In particular, this tells us that there are no absolute maxima or minima.
