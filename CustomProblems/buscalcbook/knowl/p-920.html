We are told that a change of input \(\Delta x = 100\) will result in an increase in total cost of \(\Delta C = 2000\text{.}\) To find the average change, divide that $2000 evenly across the 100 additional items

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta x} = \dfrac{2000}{100} = 20 \end{equation*}

On average, each of the 100 additional items will increase the total cost by $20.
